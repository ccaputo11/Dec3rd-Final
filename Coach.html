GUIDELINES

Challenges work best when you make them personal so make sure you continue to work on your own results so that you can be a source of inspiration and an example for your challengers to follow.

When used properly this challenge is a great tool for acquiring new customers and activating existing ones. In these guides you will find, step by step instructions on how to announce and promote this challenge as well as simple scripts you can use to contact your prospects online and offline, even if you are a brand new coach..

RESPONSIBILITIES

COACHES’ MUSTS:

  • Buy-in as early as possible to secure a larger window of time to get more clients
  • Provide Challengers with coach code for them to register to VIP ZONE
  • Collect challengers info, pictures and videos on time
  • Join coaches’ chat
  • Join and participate in challenge chat
  • Follow up ONE ON ONE with their clients outside of chat
  • SUBMIT challengers BEFORE and AFTER PICS and 360 video at the end of the challenge
  • ANSWER ANY CHALLENGE QUESTIONS in and out of the chat
  • Stay ACTIVE IN challenge CHAT
  • Must assist their challengers in collecting and submitting required information to claim prize in the case challenger is a winner.

MOTIVATION

BEGINNINGS ARE HARD

Set goals, make them public, break those goals down into smaller goals, find aspects that you like within the goals you don’t like, set rewards for crushing those goals and don’t forget you are not alone.
