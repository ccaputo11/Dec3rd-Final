GOAL SETTING

FOCUS ON HOW YOU FEEL

We often get frustrated by focusing on the menacing number on the scale, or even the percentage of fat that we are, which will absolutely destroy your expectations. You have to concentrate on how you feel. If your jeans are fitting lose or if you have extra energy to complete a specific task, motivation for weight loss, weight gain or muscle gain doesn’t have to be a tedious task, If you focus on how you feel each time you exercise, you’ll get all the benefite of burning calories, plus the reinforcement of remembering how good it felt to do it, which should increase your motivation to do more.

CONFRONT YOUR GOALS EVERY MORNING

Find something that you look forward to wearing after your reach your goals and hang it close to your mirror/door/dresser. This particular item is already in your closet so it is more likely to be a realistic goal for you. By hanging this and seeing it everyday it will help ignite your motivation to continue eating healthy and to keep hitting the gym.

SILENCE YOUR INNER CRITIC

When we begin our healthy lifestyle we tend to use self-criticism as a motivational tool to push us along. This is extremely apparent when it comes to weight loss inspiration. Self-criticism sometimes doesn’t motivate us, it can actually sabotage us and bring us down even further. Before you think about criticizing yourself, take a deep breath, close your eyes, silence all the negativity and just breathe. Once you are done go make yourself a tea and start enjoying your day.
